What We Leave Out

Pure ingredients.
Nothing hidden.

Most brands tell you what they add. We tell you what we don't.

01
No Bleach

Conventional paper is bleached white. Ours stays the natural color of bamboo. What you see is what it is.

02
No Chlorine

Chlorine processing leaves chemical residue. Your skin is too close to this product for us to accept that.

03
No BPA

No endocrine-disrupting compounds. No synthetic additives. Nothing that should not be near human skin.

04
No Dyes

Coloring agents have no place in a product used on skin this sensitive. The color you see is pure bamboo.
